Privacy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

If you subscribe for updates, we collect your email address and any other information you choose to share with us through the signup form. If you interact with our store, cart, checkout, or contact forms, certain customer, order, or technical information may also be collected through the platforms we use to operate those services.

How we use it

We use this information to send updates about STARFACE, including drops, releases, product news, and related brand announcements, and to operate, manage, and improve the website and store experience.

Email platform

We use Kit to manage subscriptions and send emails. Your information is stored through that platform and used only for the purposes described here.

Store platform

Our online store and certain commerce-related functions are powered through Shopify. Where you browse products, use the cart, begin checkout, submit store-related forms, or make a purchase, relevant personal and transactional information may be processed through Shopify and its systems in order to provide those services.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with service providers where necessary to run the website, manage email communications, operate the store, process transactions, or support related site functionality.

Your choices

  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.
  • You can request deletion of your information by emailing support@thestarface.com.

Cookies and analytics

We may use basic analytics, cookies, or similar tools to understand site and store performance and improve the experience. If our use of cookies or tracking changes materially, we will update this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your information, contact us at support@thestarface.com.

What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect your email address (and your name if you choose to share it). That’s it.

We use ConvertKit to manage the waitlist and emails. Your data is stored securely by ConvertKit and accessible to us for the purposes above.

We don’t sell your data. We only share it with trusted third-party services strictly necessary to run our email list (e.g., ConvertKit).

Your choices

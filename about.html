About

STARFACE is shaped by a fascination with collectible figures — their presence, and the way they hold attention without needing to do anything. It began as a project to develop ideas into forms that feel considered, physical, and worth keeping.

Space Kid is the first release. A starting point, not a definition. Built with an understanding of what holds value over time.

The form is simple, but deliberate. The helmet, the star eyes, the jewellery, the stillness in the stance. Each element is placed with purpose. Everything is intentional.

STARFACE sits between art and object, using character-led design to explore presence and imagination in a way that feels both minimal and expressive.

If it connects, that’s the point.

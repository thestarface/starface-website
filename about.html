About

STARFACE makes collectible figures.

Objects made to be looked at, comfortable simply being.

They don’t need to do much, yet still hold a presence you notice without trying.

Space Kid is the first of these. A simple form, with six different colourways to choose from.

All its features are intentional. What stayed, stayed for a reason.

There’s care behind how each piece is made. Not just in how it looks, but in how it lasts — something you can keep and not feel the need to replace.

This is just the beginning of a wider world. More forms, more ideas, all built in the same way.

If it connects, that’s the point.

— Femi

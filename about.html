About STARFACE™

STARFACE™ began as a sketch — an idea that lingered for years before it finally took shape. It wasn’t rushed; it just waited for the right time.

The first figure emerged from that moment of finally doing something that had been sitting quietly in the background. No big team. No grand plan. Just the quiet persistence of an idea that wouldn’t let go.

What started as a concept became something bigger — a symbol of aspiration wrapped in simplicity. STARFACE is about showing up in places people don’t always expect you to be, and doing it with intention.

We’re not trying to say too much. We just believe in things that last — things that look good, feel right, and mean something to the person who keeps them.

And the name? There’s no mystery to it. It just felt distinct — a little offbeat, a little cosmic — open enough to let the story evolve.

You can call it art, a collectible, or something in between. If it connects with you, then maybe that’s the meaning it was waiting for.

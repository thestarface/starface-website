About STARFACE

STARFACE began as a return to making.

What started as a simple act of drawing gradually became something more — a way of exploring form, character, and presence without constraint.

Over time, that process evolved into something more — creating pieces that could exist beyond the sketchbook, while still being worth keeping.

Space Kid is the first expression of that world. A collectible shaped by clarity, imagination, and the idea that objects can carry meaning beyond their form.

STARFACE sits between art and object — focused on creating pieces that feel distinct, considered, and worth holding onto.

This is only the beginning.

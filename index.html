Meet Space Kid

SPACE KID is for the dreamers and believers who refuse to let their circumstances write the ending of their story.

The star eyes are more than a design feature. They represent the choice to see possibility where others see limits. To imagine a future beyond what is directly in front of you.

The helmet represents resilience. A reminder to protect your mind and protect your vision when the world feels uncertain.

The jewelry is not about wealth or flamboyance. It is about value. A reminder that your worth is not determined by where you started.

SPACE KID represents the individual whose environment says, “Stay here,” but whose imagination says, “There is more.”

Space is possibility. Space is exploration. Space is the unknown. It’s not about where you are. It’s about where you’re going.

IKONS is our way of changing that. Each figure is inspired by the people, moments, and creative energy that continue to influence culture around the world, reimagined through the STARFACE design language.

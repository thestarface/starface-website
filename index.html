The Origin

Why Starface Exists

SpaceKid began long before the first figure was made.

After years in the corporate world, something still felt unfinished — not wrong, just incomplete. A book, Drawing on the Right Side of the Brain, reconnected something that had always been there: the instinct to create.

But creating came with a different problem. Each time a piece of art was sold, it felt like something was lost with it.

SpaceKid became the answer to that — a collectible form that could be shared without losing the original idea.

What started as a personal solution became something more: a figure built around potential, resilience, and the decision to make something lasting out of what you have.

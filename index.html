MEET SPACEKID

Starface SpaceKid is our debut collectible — a 14-inch (35.6 cm) figure with a width of 6.7 inches (17 cm), crafted from premium-quality vinyl using world-class manufacturing standards. Every detail intentionally crafted in a bold celebration of possibility, imagination, and forward motion.

Launching worldwide in January 2026 in six colour variants. Each colour variant will debut with 25 units during our initial release. After launch, each variant may receive between 100 and 125 additional units, up to a maximum of 150 pieces per variant — depending entirely on demand.

The Origin Variant presents SpaceKid in his purest form — the flagship colourway. The remaining five variants will be revealed ahead of the official drop, and early explorers will receive exclusive launch-week pricing.

SpaceKid is produced in partnership with Demeng Toy, one of the world’s leading vinyl art toy manufacturers trusted by top-tier collectible brands. Their expertise ensures that each piece meets the highest standards of craftsmanship, durability, and finish.

SpaceKid is an art collectible — not a toy. Recommended age: 14+.

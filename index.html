Origin Variant — First Look

Meet SpaceKid

Starface SpaceKid is a symbol of quiet potential — a kid from ordinary beginnings who believes there is a star inside everyone. He explores new worlds without forgetting where he comes from.

Star Eyes

His eyes are stars because he sees beyond what is in front of him — always looking for possibility, not limitation.

Helmet

The helmet is his protection as he steps into unknown spaces — a reminder that it’s okay to explore new worlds while keeping your mind guarded.

Necklace of Stars

Each star on his chain represents a talent — some he already knows, others he’s still discovering along the journey. His gifts are collected, not rushed.

Locs

His locs are a nod to his roots, his culture, and the people he comes from. No matter how far he travels, his identity travels with him.

Boots

Heavy boots keep him grounded. They’re built for long walks, slow progress and big dreams.

Silver Bracelet

The bracelet is a simple band — a quiet reminder to stay disciplined, consistent and faithful to the journey.

Backpack

His backpack carries memories, tools and ideas. Everything he needs to turn small opportunities into something cosmic.

From Sketchbook to Space

SpaceKid began as a pencil sketch — a simple idea of a kid in locs, boots and a star necklace, staring quietly into the future. This is where the journey started.
